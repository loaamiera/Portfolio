Link to the interesting website of the week.

This is a website where one can make an rgb color. This website stood out to me because sometimes when I am making a site, I have a really specific color in mind. Here, I can just click a section in the color gradient and receive the rgb for it. It allows me to bypass looking through several charts, or being confined to one shade of a color such as color: green.
