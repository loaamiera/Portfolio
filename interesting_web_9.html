Interesting Website Week 9

Link to the interesting website of the week.

This website is simply someone's blog. The reason that it is so interesting to me is because blogs of this nature typically don't exist anymore. I was surprised to stumble across a whole website dedicated to someone's ramblings when googling something else. It doesn't even really have a homepage, but once you end up on a random entry from a search engine, there is a menu where you can click on others. It also features comments from people with accounts and profile pictures, which also really surprised me.
